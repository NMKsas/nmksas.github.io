From Seafaring to Information Technology

Before my career in ITC, I have sailed international waters as a deckhand on multiple vessels. Ships represent the pinnacle of applied engineering, combining disciplines from hydraylics and automation to communication technologies in demanding maritime conditions. In 2020 my interest in autonomous shipping and nautical information technology led me to shift my career from sea to Information Technology full-time, as I started my studies in Tampere University.
